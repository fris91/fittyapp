The FittyApp logo

A friendly, soft mark built on the app's own DNA: a rounded coral tile, a warm wordmark, and a little sprout of mint — wellness, growth and gentle momentum in one glyph.

The three concepts explored

★ Chosen

Sprout F

A rounded “F” with a mint leaf budding from the top arm. Reads as wellness, nutrition & growth — the friendliest, most on-brand of the three.

Spark F

The leaf becomes a sparkle — leans into the AI-coach side. Energetic, but slightly less “wellness”, more “tech”.

Ring F

An activity ring wraps the F — ties to the Move/Meals/Body rings in the UI. Strong meaning, but busier at tiny sizes.

Why Sprout F. It carries the app's spirit in a single shape — the F keeps it unmistakably FittyApp, the leaf says wellness & nutrition, the upward growth says progress, and the rounded, soft forms match the pastel, encouraging tone. It stays legible all the way down to a 16px favicon.
